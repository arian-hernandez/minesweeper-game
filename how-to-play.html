How to Play Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper! Here are the basics:

  • Left-click on a cell to reveal it.
  • Right-click to place a flag on suspected mines.
  • Reveal all non-mine cells to win!
  • If you click on a mine, you lose.

Numbers on revealed cells show how many mines are adjacent (including diagonals).
